Our Story

Toree Farm and Nursery Limited was established to bridge the gap between quality seed production and farmers needing reliable vegetable seedlings. Located in Litein Town, behind AIC Litein Hospital, we focus exclusively on vegetable varieties and apply professional nursery management practices.

Our approach includes controlled irrigation, soil enrichment, proper spacing, and rigorous pest management. Every seedling undergoes careful monitoring from germination through to hardening, ensuring it is strong and ready for transplanting with minimal shock.

We serve small-scale farmers, commercial growers, and agribusiness partners with consistent supply, fair pricing, and professional support.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our seedlings and services.

How long do seedlings take to be ready for transplanting?

Most vegetable varieties are ready for transplanting within 4-6 weeks from germination, depending on the crop type and growing conditions. We monitor each tray daily and only sell when seedlings are at optimal transplanting size.

Do you offer bulk discounts for large orders?

Yes! We offer competitive bulk pricing for commercial orders. Contact us directly for custom quotes based on your volume requirements. We work with smallholder farmers, cooperatives, and large-scale commercial growers.

How do you ensure seedling quality?

Quality control is our top priority. We use certified seeds, sterilized growing media, controlled irrigation, preventive pest management, and rigorous inspection before every sale. Our germination success rate exceeds 98%.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, and cash payments. For larger orders, we can arrange flexible payment terms. All transactions are secure and receipts are provided for your records.

Do you provide delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery within Kericho County and surrounding areas. Delivery fees vary by location and order size. We also provide loading assistance for customers who prefer to pick up their orders.

What makes Toree Farm different from other nurseries?

We specialize exclusively in vegetable seedlings and apply professional nursery management practices. Our controlled environment, expert staff, and commitment to quality ensure consistent results for farmers.
