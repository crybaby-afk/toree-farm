Toree Farm and Nursery Limited specializes exclusively in high-quality vegetable seedlings. Using sustainable nursery practices and strict quality control, we produce strong, well-hardened seedlings ready to thrive in your farm or garden.

Located in Litein Town, behind AIC Litein Hospital, we serve both small-scale farmers and large commercial growers across the region with consistent supply and professional service.
